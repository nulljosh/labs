Demo — sample content plan

A quick run of the SEO content pipeline on a placeholder niche (no real client site yet), to show what a monthly content plan looks like before work starts.

Example niche: residential HVAC repair, Austin TX

Research finding: top-ranking local competitors lean on certifications, 24/7 response, and pricing/reviews — almost none publish real diagnostic/educational content. That's the content gap.

Pillar: "Austin AC Repair Cost & Troubleshooting Guide" — common problems, real repair cost ranges, DIY checks vs. calling a pro, how to vet a licensed contractor, FAQ.

Supporting pages: "Why Is My AC Blowing Warm Air?" · "AC Not Turning On? 7 Causes" · "What Counts as an Emergency AC Repair?" — each links back to the pillar.

Same process runs against the real site/niche once work starts — pillar + supporting articles per topic cluster, or one page per city for multi-location GBPs.
