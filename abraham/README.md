# Abraham

SEO subcontracting + a cold email bot, both for Abraham. Two jobs, one contract, one repo.

## Job 1 — SEO / Google Business Profile retainer

- **Website build:** $500 one-time
- **Monthly retainer:** $500/mo per Google Business Profile — site work, service/location pages, GBP optimization, backlinks, content, rank monitoring, technical SEO, monthly report
- Priced below standard market ($750-1,500/mo for the same scope) on the bet that more profiles come online if this one performs. Bumps to $750/mo after 90 days if that scale doesn't materialize.

## Job 2 — Cold email bot

- **Rate:** $45/hr, ~4-8 hours
- Reads a lead list, drafts a personalized email per lead with Claude, sends it. No CRM, no dashboard.
- Code lives in `src/` — currently scaffolding only (`leads.py`, `writer.py`, `sender.py`, `main.py`), not wired to Abraham's real lead format/email account yet.

## Before work starts

Nothing begins on either job until:
1. The contract is signed by both sides
2. The first month's payment is sent (e-transfer)

No signature + no payment = no work.
